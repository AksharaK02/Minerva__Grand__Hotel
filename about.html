About Minerva Grand Hotel

Minerva Grand Hotel is a modern luxury hotel designed to provide guests with comfort, elegance, and exceptional hospitality. Located in Hyderabad, our hotel welcomes business travelers, families, and tourists with premium accommodation and personalized services.

Our Story

Since its establishment, Minerva Grand Hotel has been dedicated to creating memorable experiences for every guest. We combine traditional hospitality with modern amenities to ensure a relaxing and enjoyable stay. Our commitment to quality service has made us a preferred destination for visitors seeking comfort and convenience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide world-class hospitality by offering luxurious rooms, delicious dining, modern facilities, and friendly customer service. We strive to exceed guest expectations through professionalism, cleanliness, and attention to detail.

Our Facilities

  • Luxury Deluxe, Premium and Suite Rooms
  • Fine Dining Restaurant
  • Free High-Speed Wi-Fi
  • 24×7 Front Desk Support
  • Conference & Meeting Halls
  • Swimming Pool
  • Free Parking Facility
  • Room Service

Why Choose Us?

Guests choose Minerva Grand Hotel for its comfortable accommodation, professional staff, convenient location, affordable luxury, and excellent customer service. Whether you are visiting Hyderabad for business or leisure, we are committed to making your stay comfortable and memorable.
